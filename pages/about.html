About us

Welcome to TAMURAKOMA VIETNAM

Present in Vietnam since 2007 as a representative office, Tamurakoma Vietnam started to promote garment business between Vietnam and Japan. After nearly 10 years of accumulating experience and relationships in Vietnam, Tamurakoma Vietnam Co., Ltd. was born. With our business outside Japan market, we also expanded our business portfolio from simply garment to materials of fabrics, bags, medical protective goods.

Our Business

Tamurakoma Vietnam is a trading company specializing in garment-related products such as ready-made clothes, fabrics, and accessories. Currently, we trade in other products such as bags, medical protective equipment. The main markets are East Asia (80%), Europe (10%), North America (8%), and domestic (2%).

History of TAMURAKOMA & CO., Ltd.

  • 1894-1898

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The first Tamura Komajiro founded "Kouda-ya Tamura Shoten" in 1894. We were particular about the products that are unique to Tamurakoma, and launched products one after another. Western goods such as muslin dominated the industry. In 1898, a "design room" was set up to strengthen design and planning capabilities. It made a name for itself as "Design of Tamurakoma".

  • 1918-1931

    The expansion to global market

    Established TAMURAKOMA Shoten Co., Ltd. in 1918. During the financial crisis of the 1920s, as the domestic market cooled, it sought overseas sales channels.
    Sales channels are pioneered in Indonesia, Thailand and Singapore.

  • 1985

    Focus shift to commercialization and overseas production

    From the bubble economy to the Heisei recession. Tamurakoma faces a major turning point in the turbulent and difficult-to-read era. One was the strategic strengthening of secondary products, therefore we tried to break away from the fabric business. On the other hand, production is shifting overseas.
    Starting with the opening of an office in Shanghai in 1985, Beijing, Seoul and Taipei (1986), Tamurakoma (Hong Kong) Co., Ltd. was established in 1994, and Tamurakoma (Shanghai) Textiles Co., Ltd. was established in 2001.

  • 2007

    Opening of TAMURAKOMA HOCHIMINH REPRESENTATIVE OFFICE

    Marking Tamurakoma & Co., Ltd's presence in Vietnam, Tamurakoma Ho Chi Minh representative office initially played the role of commercial connection, quality, and delivery time management for the parent company in Japan. From the original items of medical uniforms and labor protection products, Tamurakoma Vietnam gradually diversified into fashion and clothing products. Tamurakoma Vietnam always has a QC team directly supervising the sewing lines and the MD team as the liaison between the factories and the parent company in Japan. The main activities of the representative office are at receiving orders from salespeople in Japan and monitoring production in Vietnam.

  • 2016

    TAMURAKOMA VIETNAM Co.,Ltd is established

    With the ambition to expand beyond the field of order management and do business with more markets outside of Japan, Tamurakoma Vietnam Limted Company was established, on the basis of independent operation with the representative office in Ho Chi Minh City. Today, Tamurakoma Vietnam does not stop at the production control of the garment and clothing industry, but also expands into the trading business of fabrics, bags, medical protective goods, and environmentally friendly products.

  • 2020

    TAMURAKOMA VIETNAM EXPANDS GLOBALLY BUSINESS

    Besides traditional partners in Japan. Tamurakoma Vietnam also expands ours market to the US and Europe with a more diversified product portfolio.
