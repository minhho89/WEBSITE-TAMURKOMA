History of TAMURAKOMA

  • 1894-1898

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The first Tamura Komajiro founded "Kouda-ya Tamura Shoten" in 1894. We were particular about the products that are unique to Tamurakoma, and launched products one after another. Western goods such as muslin dominated the industry. In 1898, a "design room" was set up to strengthen design and planning capabilities. It made a name for itself as "Design of Tamurakoma".

  • 1918-1931

    The expansion to global market

    Established TAMURAKOMA Shoten Co., Ltd. in 1918. During the financial crisis of the 1920s, as the domestic market cooled, it sought overseas sales channels.
    Sales channels are pioneered in Indonesia, Thailand and Singapore.

  • 1985

    Focus shift to commercialization and overseas production

    From the bubble economy to the Heisei recession. Tamurakoma faces a major turning point in the turbulent and difficult-to-read era. One was the strategic strengthening of secondary products, therefore we tried to break away from the fabric business. On the other hand, production is shifting overseas.
    Starting with the opening of an office in Shanghai in 1985, Beijing, Seoul and Taipei (1986), Tamurakoma (Hong Kong) Co., Ltd. was established in 1994, and Tamurakoma (Shanghai) Textiles Co., Ltd. was established in 2001.
